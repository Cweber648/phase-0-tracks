Release 0: Equip Your New Robot
How to use an elevator.

Go in elevator:
    -When wall is reached, scan for buttons
    - If buttons found: 
        -Read button labels one by one until desired number is found.
        -When button is found: press it.
        -Wait until doors open on desired floor.
        -When doors open: exit the elevator.
    - If no buttons, move to next wall, and call for ‘buttons found’ method.


Release 1: Find a Real-Life Algorithm
How to separate light/white clothes and dark clothes for doing laundry.

Pick one piece of laundry until hamper is empty:
    -Is item light or dark? 
    -If clothes is light:
        -Put into left pile.
    -Else, put into right pile.
End loop.
