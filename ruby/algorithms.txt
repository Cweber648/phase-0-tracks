*Push the up or down button to call the elevator (Top button is Up, Bottom button is Down)
* Step into the elevator (door will close automatically after 5 seconds)
* Turn and face the buttons to see what possible floor options.
* Pending how many floors and columns of buttons the very top button will be the highest floor.
* Lowest button will be for the lowest possible floor
* If you are going to floor 2. Push the button that has "2" on it.
*If you have not learned how to read yet. It will be the second button on the column of elevator buttons.
*Wait until the elevator begins to move
*Once elevator has stopped moving. The doors will open.
*Once doors open, step outside of elevator at your desired floor.
